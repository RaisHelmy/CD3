Thanks for using this app. Please go through this tutorial before using the application

1. Click button on the left side

From the picture, we can see there is a line there separating left side and right side. The left side colored with pink shows as input. Click any note at left side to start.

2. Adjust the line

The line that separating left side and right side is movable. You can drag left to decrease the input size and to the right to increase the input size. Just click the line to left or the right.

3. Click refresh/generate button to generate music

The rotating like pink button that attached to the line is a button that will generate music from the input

Generated music with green color is generated and running by auto

After you generated a music, a green paterned will be displayed. The blue color will be moving and running to show current played music.

4. Click pause button/ play button

Click pause button to stop running music and click play again if you want to run the music again.

5. Change music pattern

You may change music pattern size by clicking dropdown menu.

6. Change music tempo

Use first slider, and slide it to your preferred tempo like this

7. Change music swing

Use second slider, and slide it to your preferred swing like this

8. Change music temperature

Use third slider, and slide it to your preferred temperature like this
